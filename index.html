Hello

Improvised lo-fi beats meet freestyle rap in the lioncity’s first and only hiphop podcast with each episode featuring new up and coming local talents

OURGROOVE:GOODVIBES

Here at Bro-Fi we focus on taking a jam-centric approach towards making music, creating a safe space where artists are able to vibe and groove with us. There will be mistakes, there will be laughs, and that is integral to what we stand for

Episodes

Episode 6 ft. MC 120

Underground Freestyle Rapper MC 120 blows the roof off with his mad skills. We rumble, we battle, and hop on some Studio Ghibli vibes. This episode is absolute fire!

Episode 5 - Bro-Fi Special | Thinkin''bout Maplestory

What does Maplestory, one of the most popular MMORPG of the early 2000's, have to do with Bro-Fi? On this special episode, we reveal how Bro-Fi came to be, and how our lives intertwine with the game we just can't stop thinkin bout.

Episode 4 ft. BGourd

Come chill out with the 6th best rapper in Singapore as he reveals his origin story. BGourd shares his experiences of performing with the green suit, and breaks down his hit single "Fairprice" for us. We guarantee a vegetastic time!

Episode 3 ft. Josh Tan

On this episode, we talk about the Bro-Fi core values and overconfident music teachers with Singapore and Melbourne based songwriter/guitarist Josh Tan. Listen to an unreleased tune of his [Going, Gone], that was written a decade ago.

Episode 2 ft. Nikhil Senan

We drink beer, spit fire and retract our landing gear. Taking flight with rapper Nikhil Senan, as he demystifies his approach to rap and hip hop.

Episode 1 ft. Bobbi Brown

Come hang as we freestyle and chill out with up and coming local rapper Bobbi Brown, in our pilot episode of Bro-Fi.
